Séance is an Uncommon Effect Joker that gives a Spectral Card whenever a Straight Flush is played. This also includes Royal Flush.

Séance cannot provide The Soul (Creates a [[Legendary Jokers|{{hl|purple|Legendary}}]] Joker <br> {{room}} Available from start. Spectral The Soul.png) or Black Hole (Upgrade every [[Poker Hands|{{hl|purple|poker hand}}]] by {{hl|orange|1}} level Available from start. Spectral Black Hole.png), as both can only be found in Booster Packs.

Synergies

[edit | edit source]

Séance is a bit of an odd Joker, as very few Spectral cards benefit from full Straight Flush builds. It is best used as a temporary Spectral generator that does not have limited uses, like Sixth Sense (If {{hl|orange|first hand}} of round is a single {{hl|orange|6}}, destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|{{hl|sblue|Spectral}}]] card <br> {{room}} Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Sixth_Sense.png) does, to create useful Spectrals that can then facilitate other builds. Anything that benefits from certain Spectral cards, theoretically, has some synergy with Séance.

For this reason, the synergies listed are split into two sections: ways to make more Straight Flushes, to maximize the Spectral cards Séance can give; and ways to use those Spectral cards to their maximum effect.

Decks

[edit | edit source]
  • Checkered Deck ( Start run with {{hl|orange|26}} {{suit|Spades}} and {{hl|orange|26}} {{suit|Hearts}} in deck Win a run with the {{D|Black}} on any difficulty Checkered Deck.png) is by far the best one to use Séance, as it is much easier to create Straight Flushes with fewer differing suits.
  • Red Deck ( {{hl|red|+1}} discard every round Unlocked from start Red Deck.png), Blue Deck ( {{hl|blue|+1}} hand every round Discover at least {{hl|orange|20}} items from your [[collection]] Blue Deck.png), and Painted Deck ( {{hl|orange|+2}} hand size, <br> {{hl|red|-1}} Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] on the {{Stake|Green}} difficulty or harder Painted Deck.png) all make it slightly easier to find Straight Flushes.
  • Magic Deck ( Start run with the {{V|Crystal Ball|class=hl-purple}} voucher and {{hl|orange|2}} copies of {{tarot|The Fool}} Win a run with the {{D|Red}} on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] Magic Deck.png)'s Fool cards are incredibly helpful, as they can be used to create a Suit-changing Tarot that can be used in a round to guarantee a Straight Flush. Starting with an extra consumable slot is also great for holding more Spectral cards.
  • 15 Minute City ( ) starts with an Eternal Shortcut (Allows {{ph|Straights}} to be made with gaps of {{hl|orange|1 rank}} <br> {{hl|gray|(ex: {{hl|orange|10 8 6 5 3}})}} Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Shortcut.png), which allows Straight Flushes to be made with gaps in them, making it easier to get that hand.

Poker Hands

[edit | edit source]

Straight Flushes (and Royal Flushes) are the only hands relevant to this Joker.

Helping Séance

  • Four Fingers (All {{ph|Flushes}} and {{ph|Straights}} can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Four Fingers.png) and Shortcut (Allows {{ph|Straights}} to be made with gaps of {{hl|orange|1 rank}} <br> {{hl|gray|(ex: {{hl|orange|10 8 6 5 3}})}} Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Shortcut.png) both make Straight Flushes easier to play, allowing Séance to trigger more often.   • DNA (If {{hl|orange|first hand}} of round has only {{hl|orange|1}} card, add a permanent copy to deck and draw it to {{hl|orange|hand}} Rare Effect On Played DNA.png) can be used to copy better cards, as Séance can create the Aura (Add [[Foil|{{hl|edition|Foil}}]], [[Holographic|{{hl|edition|Holographic}}]], or [[Polychrome|{{hl|edition|Polychrome}}]] effect to {{hl|orange|1}} selected card in hand Available from start. Vouchers

[edit | edit source]
  • Grabber (Permanently gain {{hl|blue|+1}} hand per round Base Grabber.png) and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional {{hl|blue|+1}} hand per round Play a total of 2,500 cards Upgraded Nacho Tong.png) both give +1 hand, allowing the player to play more Straight Flushes and generate more Spectral Cards.
  • Wasteful (Permanently gain {{hl|red|+1}} discard each round Base Wasteful.png) and Recyclomancy (Permanently gain an additional {{hl|red|+1}} discard each round Discard a total of 2500 cards Upgraded Recyclomancy.png) both give +1 discard, allowing the player to search for Straight Flushes easier.
  • Paint Brush ({{hl|orange|+1}} hand size Base Paint Brush.png) and Palette ({{hl|orange|+1}} hand size Reduce your hand size down to five cards. Upgraded Palette.png) both give +1 hand size, which can help the player to form Straight Flushes easier.

Anti-synergies

[edit | edit source]

Decks

[edit | edit source]
  • Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|{{hl|orange|Ranks}}]] and [[Suits|{{hl|orange|Suits}}]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] on the {{Stake|Orange}} difficulty or harder Erratic Deck.png) can make it difficult to use Séance, as the randomized rank and suit makes it harder to form Straight Flushes.
  • Black Deck ( {{hl|orange|+1}} Joker slot <br> {{hl|blue|-1}} hand every round Discover at least {{hl|orange|100}} items from your [[collection]] Black Deck.png) has slight anti-synergy, as 1 less available hand makes it slightly harder to find Straight Flushes. However, it is easier to take Séance, as it is less likely to take up a crucial Joker slot needed for survival.

Blinds

[edit | edit source]
  • The Manacle ( Boss The Manacle.png)'s -1 hand size makes it hard to assemble Straight Flushes.
  • The Water ( Boss The Water.png) will always start with 0 discards, making it very hard to assemble Straight Flushes.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • A séance is a traditional method for communicating with spirits or the dead. Often, but not necessarily, a group is seated around a table with a medium leading the ritual.
  • Séance is the only Joker in Balatro that uses a letter with a diacritic. The letter é is an e with an acute accent, or l'accent aigu in French.
