Usage Notes

[edit | edit source]
  • The next reroll still starts at the same price it would have been without the extra reroll, so Chaos the Clown effectively saves $ equal to Rerolls+4.
    • With Reroll Surplus (Rerolls cost {{Money|2}} less Base Reroll Surplus.png), this becomes Savings=Rerolls+2, and with Reroll Glut (Rerolls cost an additional {{Money|2}} less Reroll the shop a total of 100 times Upgraded Reroll Glut.png), it becomes Savings=Rerolls.
  • If Chaos the Clown is obtained while in a shop, the price for the current shop reroll will be brought down to $0, regardless of whether it is the first reroll in that shop or not. The next reroll will cost where it previously left off.
  • If the player sells Chaos the Clown whilst the once-per-shop free reroll is active, the reroll cost will immediately revert to the regular value.
  • As Chaos the Clown costs $4 but a standard re-roll costs $5, it can be cheaper to buy him to reroll the shop, and the player can sell him again later for an extra $2 back.
    • If Chaos the Clown is sold after using its one free reroll and another one is found, it can be purchased again for another free reroll.
  • Multiple copies of Chaos the Clown (obtained through Ankh (Create a copy of a random [[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]], destroy all other Jokers <br> {{hl|gray|(Removes {{hl|edition|Negative}} from copy)}} Available from start. Spectral Ankh.png), Invisible Joker (After {{hl|orange|2}} rounds, sell this card to {{hl|orange|Duplicate}} a random Joker <br> {{currently|{{hl|orange|0}}/2}} <br> {{hl|gray|(Removes [[Negative|{{hl|edition|Negative}}]] from copy)}} Win a run without ever having more than {{hl|orange|4 Jokers}} Rare Effect N/A (Passive) Invisible Joker.png), or Showman ([[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]], [[Tarot Cards|{{hl|purple|Tarot}}]], [[Planet Cards|{{hl|cyan|Planet}}]], and [[Spectral Cards|{{hl|sblue|Spectral}}]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level {{hl|orange|4}} Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Showman.png)) do stack, allowing the player to get 2 free rerolls.

Trivia

[edit | edit source]
  • The name and art for this joker is presumably based on Krusty the Clown, a character from the animated sitcom television series The Simpsons.
