Uncommon Jokers

From Balatro Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Uncommon Jokers are a type of Joker. A Joker has a 25% chance of being a Uncommon Joker when generated randomly.

Uncommon Jokers can be ensured using:

  • Uncommon Tag ( Uncommon tag.png), which creates a free Uncommon Joker in the next shop.

They also have special synergy with Baseball Card ([[Uncommon Jokers|{{hl|green|Uncommon}}]] Jokers each give {{xmult|1.5}} Rare Multiplicative Mult On Other Jokers Baseball Card.png), which provides X1.5 Mult for each Uncommon Joker owned.
