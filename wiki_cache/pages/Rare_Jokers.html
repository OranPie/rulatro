Rare Jokers

From Balatro Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Rare Jokers are the least common type of Joker available by normal means (such as the Shop and Judgement (Creates a random [[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]] card<br>{{room}} Available from start. Tarot Judgement.png)). A Joker has a 5% chance of being a Rare Joker.

Rare Jokers can be ensured using:

  • Wraith (Creates a random [[Rare Jokers|{{hl|red|Rare}} {{hl|orange|Joker}}]], sets money to {{$|0}} Available from start. Spectral Wraith.png), which creates a random Rare Joker but sets money to $0.
  • Rare Tag ( Discover {{J|Blueprint}} Rare tag.png), which creates a free Rare Joker in the next shop.
