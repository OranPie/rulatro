Common Jokers

From Balatro Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Common Jokers are the most common type of Joker. A Joker has a 70% chance of being a Common Joker when generated randomly.

Most of them are unlocked from the very start of a save file. They typically have easy to reach conditions, but relatively weak effects.

Common Jokers can be ensured using:

  • Riff-Raff (When [[Blind|{{hl|orange|Blind}}]] is selected, create {{hl|orange|2}} [[Common Jokers|{{hl|blue|Common}} {{hl|orange|Jokers}}]] <br> {{room}} Common Effect On Blind Select Riff-Raff.png), which creates two Common Jokers at the beginning of a round.
  • Top-up Tag ( Top-up tag.png) Tag, which creates two Common Jokers upon skipping.
