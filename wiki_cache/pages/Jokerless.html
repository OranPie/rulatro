Restrictions
All methods of acquiring Jokers:
  • Tarot Cards: Judgement (Creates a random [[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]] card<br>{{room}} Available from start. Tarot Judgement.png)
  • Spectral Cards: Wraith (Creates a random [[Rare Jokers|{{hl|red|Rare}} {{hl|orange|Joker}}]], sets money to {{$|0}} Available from start. Spectral Wraith.png) and The Soul (Creates a [[Legendary Jokers|{{hl|purple|Legendary}}]] Joker <br> {{room}} Available from start. Spectral The Soul.png)
  • Tags: Uncommon Tag ( Uncommon tag.png), Rare Tag ( Discover {{J|Blueprint}} Rare tag.png), Negative Tag ( Discover the {{edition|Negative}} edition Negative tag.png), Foil Tag ( Discover the {{edition|Foil}} edition Foil tag.png), Holographic Tag ( Discover the {{edition|Holographic}} edition Holographic tag.png), Polychrome Tag ( Discover the {{edition|Polychrome}} edition Polychrome tag.png), Buffoon Tag ( Buffoon tag.png), Top-up Tag ( Top-up tag.png)
  • Blinds: Crimson Heart ( Showdown Crimson Heart.png), Verdant Leaf ( Showdown Verdant Leaf.png), Amber Acorn ( Showdown Amber Acorn.png)
  • Vouchers: Antimatter ({{hl|edition|+1}} Joker Slot Redeem Blank 10 total times Upgraded Antimatter.png)
  • Booster Packs: all 4 Buffoon Packs

Jokerless is a Challenge Deck. The player is unable to acquire Jokers and must rely on Planet Cards, card modifications through Tarot Cards and Spectral Cards, and Vouchers for scoring.

Starting Contents

[edit | edit source]

The starting deck contains the standard 52 cards.

Synergies

[edit | edit source]

Vouchers

[edit | edit source]
  • Telescope ([[Booster Packs|{{hl|orange|Celestial Packs}}]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|{{hl|cyan|Planet}}]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|{{hl|orange|poker hand}}]] Base Telescope.png) improves the efficiency of shop purchases by ensuring the player will receive a card of their chosen hand type.
  • Observatory ([[Planet Cards|{{hl|cyan|Planet}}]] cards in your [[Consumables|{{hl|orange|consumable}}]] area give {{xmult|1.5}} for their specified [[Poker Hands|{{hl|orange|poker hand}}]] Use a total of {{hl|orange|25}} [[Planet|{{hl|cyan|Planet}}]] cards from any {{hl|cyan|Celestial Pack}} Upgraded Observatory.png) provides X1.5 Mult per planet card held in the consumable area, which will greatly increase scoring due to the lack of XMult available.
    • Crystal Ball ({{hl|orange|+1}} consumable slot Base Crystal Ball.png) further synergizes with this by offering an additional consumable slot, allowing the player to hold more planet cards.

Modifiers

[edit | edit source]
  • Blue Seals (Creates the {{Planet|}} card for final played [[Poker Hands|{{hl|orange|poker hand}}]] of round if {{hl|orange|held}} in hand<br>{{room}} Blue Seal.png) provide the most consistent source of scaling without Jokers.
  • Purple Seals (Creates a {{Tarot|}} card when {{hl|orange|discarded}}<br>{{room}} Purple Seal.png) provide a source of Tarot Cards without Jokers.
  • Gold Cards ({{money|3}} if this card is held in hand at end of round Gold Card.png) and Gold Seals (Earn {{money|3}} when this card is played and scores Gold Seal.png) provide one of the only ways to gain more money without economy Jokers.
  • Steel Cards ({{xmult|1.5}} while this card stays in hand Steel Card.png) provide a consistent way to score more without Jokers.
  • Glass Cards ({{xmult|2}}<br>{{hl|green|1 in 4}} chance to destroy card Glass Card.png) provides both a way to deck fix by breaking cards early, a quick way to score more in tough Boss Blinds or when Antes scale too quickly, and an incredibly powerful source of XMult late game.
  • Red Seals (Retrigger this card {{hl|orange|1}} time Red Seal.png) synergize with any of: Gold Cards ({{money|3}} if this card is held in hand at end of round Gold Card.png), Steel Cards ({{xmult|1.5}} while this card stays in hand Steel Card.png), and Glass Cards ({{xmult|2}}<br>{{hl|green|1 in 4}} chance to destroy card Glass Card.png).

Editions

[edit | edit source]
  • All editions on playing cards, while rare, are far more powerful without scoring Jokers, making Standard Packs and Aura (Add [[Foil|{{hl|edition|Foil}}]], [[Holographic|{{hl|edition|Holographic}}]], or [[Polychrome|{{hl|edition|Polychrome}}]] effect to {{hl|orange|1}} selected card in hand Available from start. Spectral Aura.png) even more valuable.

Poker Hands

[edit | edit source]
  • Straight is the most consistent hand to win, as it is both able to be played early game without any deck fixing, and Saturn (Increases {{ph|Straight}} hand value by {{mult|+3}} and {{chips|+30}} Available from start. Planet Saturn.png) has great scaling for late game.

Consumables

[edit | edit source]
  • Without Jokers taking up most of the slots in the shop, Tarot Cards like Death (Select {{hl|orange|2}} cards, convert the {{hl|orange|left}} card into the {{hl|orange|right}} card<br>{{hl|gray|(Drag to rearrange)}} Available from start. Tarot Death.png) and The Hanged Man (Destroys up to {{hl|orange|2}} selected cards Available from start. Tarot The Hanged Man.png) appear far more frequently. This can make deck fixing far easier.

Anti-Synergies

[edit | edit source]
  • Several Tarot Cards and Spectral Cards are still available which have no effect, such as The Wheel of Fortune ({{hl|green|1 in 4}} chance to add [[Foil|{{hl|edition|Foil}}]], [[Holographic|{{hl|edition|Holographic}}]], or [[Polychrome|{{hl|edition|Polychrome}}]] edition to a random [[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]] Available from start. Tarot The Wheel of Fortune.png), Temperance (Gives the total sell value of all current [[Jokers]]<br>{{hl|grey|(Max of}} {{hl|orange|{{Money|50}}}}{{hl|grey|)}} Available from start. Tarot Temperance.png), Ectoplasm (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|{{hl|edition|Negative}}]] to a random {{hl|orange|Joker,}} {{hl|red|-1}} hand size Available from start. Spectral Ectoplasm.png), Ankh (Create a copy of a random [[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]], destroy all other Jokers <br> {{hl|gray|(Removes {{hl|edition|Negative}} from copy)}} Available from start. Spectral Ankh.png), and Hex (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|{{hl|edition|Polychrome}}]] to a random [[Jokers|{{hl|orange|Joker}}]], destroy all other Jokers Available from start. Spectral Hex.png).
  • Blank ({{hl|gray|Does nothing?}} Base Blank.png) is still available but does nothing since Antimatter ({{hl|edition|+1}} Joker Slot Redeem Blank 10 total times Upgraded Antimatter.png) is banned and Jokers can't be purchased anyways.
  • Hone ({{hl|edition|Foil}}, {{hl|edition|Holographic}}, and {{hl|edition|Polychrome}} cards appear {{hl|orange|2X}} more often Base Hone.png) and Glow Up ({{hl|edition|Foil}}, {{hl|edition|Holographic}}, and {{hl|edition|Polychrome}} cards appear {{hl|orange|4X}} more often Have at least 5 Joker cards with Foil, Holographic, or Polychrome (or Negative) effect Upgraded Glow Up.png) are less useful because Jokers are unobtainable, but the Editioned Playing Cards from Standard Packs can still be useful.
